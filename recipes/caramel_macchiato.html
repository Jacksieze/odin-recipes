Caramel Macchiato

Espresso based with steamed milk and caramel sauce

    Requirements
  • Grinded coffee bean - 20g
  • Milk - 180ml
  • Vanilla syrup - 20g
  • Caramel sauce - 20g
    Steps
  1. Extract 25ml of espresso using Grinded coffee bean
  2. Steam milk between 60~70 Celcius.
  3. Pour espresso first in a cup, then mix 20g of vanilla syrup
  4. Pour over steamed milk on espresso.
  5. Drizzle over 20g of caramel sauce on prepared coffee
  6. Enjoy your Caramel Macchiato!
