Cafe Latte

Espresso based with steamed milk

    Requirements
  • Grinded coffee bean - 20g
  • Milk - 200ml
    Steps
  1. Extract 25ml of espresso using Grinded coffee bean
  2. Steam milk between 60~70 Celcius.
  3. Pour espresso first in a cup, then pour over steamed milk on espresso.
  4. Enjoy your Cafe Latte!
